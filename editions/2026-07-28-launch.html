The world’s rules, encoded — Axiom is now public

Axiom builds open, machine-readable encodings of statutes, regulations, and policy rules — versioned, source-cited, and tested — with a compiler, runtime, and API to execute them. Today everything goes public.

Explore

Start with the encodings

Every rule is a versioned YAML artifact with statutory citations and companion tests, from federal statutes to state regulations — browse them at axiom-foundation.org or on GitHub.
