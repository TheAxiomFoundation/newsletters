The rules that decide who gets food assistance, health coverage, and tax credits live in closed code that no one can check — re-implemented privately inside every system that needs them.

On Tuesday, July 28, the Axiom Foundation goes public to change that — and we’d love for you to be there.

The Axiom Foundation publishes the open, verifiable layer for computable law: statutes, regulations, and policy rules turned into cited, time-aware, executable code anyone can run, audit, or reform.

We’ll walk through the platform, show how every encoded rule traces back to statute, and take your questions.
